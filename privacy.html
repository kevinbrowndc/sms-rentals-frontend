RentalSMS (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates an SMS-based notification service that provides rental listing alerts to users who opt in.

Information We Collect

When you sign up for RentalSMS, we may collect your phone number and rental preferences such as city and price range. This information is collected solely to deliver SMS alerts that match your criteria.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to send SMS notifications you have opted in to receive, respond to support requests, and maintain the reliability of the service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

SMS Communications

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive recurring SMS messages from RentalSMS. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or email support@rentalsms.com.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. Once opted out, you will no longer receive messages unless you opt back in.
